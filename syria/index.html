"To start with the violence wasn’t so extreme. We could cope. But now they’ve started to kill children."

Rima is a single grandmother who was living with her daughter and grandson before her village was attacked and her daughter killed. Now, she has no choice but to flee with her grandson. The only question now is, where can they turn to?

"In my village there are a lot of houses being destroyed by air strikes, shells, tanks. Not one house is left untouched."

As Rima, you are now responsible for your grandchild and navigating the dangerous territory of Syria from Affrin in the Aleppo governorate.

Hoda, Aadam, Bilal, Sora, & Mohamad used to live comfortably in their home in, As Salamiyah, the Syrian city in the Hama governorate. A recent attack has left the family with no choice but to become displaced within their own country. The only question now is where can they turn to?

"Our neighbor’s house was completely destroyed and ours was very badly damaged. I was so afraid. I had to run out of the house. At 5am, I left with my children."

As Hoda, you are now responsible for saving your family fleeing from the Hama governorate. Where do you turn?
